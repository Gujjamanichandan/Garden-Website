Plants in our garden

Plants in M.C Garden are a collection of various types of plants, each with its own unique characteristics and requirements. These plants are essential for the survival and growth of the garden, and they play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of the ecosystem.

Vines

Vines are climbing plants that use their tendrils to attach to a support and climb upwards.

Flowering Shrubs

Flowering shrubs are small, woody plants that produce flowers. They are often characterized by their small.

Evergreens

Evergreens are coniferous trees that retain their leaves throughout the year.

Annuals

Annuals are plants that complete their life cycle in a single year. They are often characterized by their short lifespan.

Trees

Trees are large, woody plants that have a single trunk and multiple branches. They are often characterized by their large, green leaves.
